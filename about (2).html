We Build Autonomy That Works When Reality Breaks

INDE is developing the Autonomy OS for unmanned systems operating in GPS-denied, comms-degraded, and contested environments. Defense-grade by intent. Dual-use by design.

Our Mission

Building autonomous systems that adapt, learn, and survive — even when GPS fails, communications degrade, and conventional approaches break down.

🎯

Why We Exist

Current autonomy systems fail when they need to work most — in contested, GPS-denied environments. We're building the Autonomy OS that thrives precisely where others fail.

🛡️

What We Build

Defense-grade autonomous systems software for ground, air, and maritime platforms. Field-proven. Multi-sensor fusion. Graceful degradation. No GPS required.

🌍

Our Approach

Dual-use by design, compliant with EU export control regulations. Human oversight maintained. Vendor-agnostic architecture. Open to integration.
